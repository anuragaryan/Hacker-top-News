How does it work?

Every 15 minutes, Hacker-top-News scrapes the first page of Hacker News and generates a new page containing the top stories from the past 24 hours sorted in descending order based on overall points it received.

Why should I use it?

Hacker News is very famous platform for technology related news. But since it keep getting updated through out the day, we might miss some cool things. So therefore comes Hacker-top-News, it sorts out the top news from last 24 hours in the descending order of points it received so that you won't miss a thing.
